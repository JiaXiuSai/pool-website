Objectives and goals

Competition

Provide a fair and competitive environment for students to improve and learn. More importantly, students get to have fun and learn about how to interact with different cultures.

Equipment

Provide access to appropriate and necessary facilities and equipment for all members to use and play pool. The tables are maintained and kept a high standard so the students can excel in the game.

Social

Provide opportunities for students to be involved in the social life of the college and university. Balancing your time between work and play is an important skill for students to learn before entering the workforce.
